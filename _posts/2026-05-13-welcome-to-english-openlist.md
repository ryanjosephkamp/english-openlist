# Welcome to the English OpenList Blog

**We're thrilled to launch the official English OpenList blog!**

After months of building the largest open-source, Scrabble-compatible English word list (now with **378,666+ validated words**), we're excited to share our journey with you in a new way.

## What You'll Find Here

Every day at 00:00 UTC, our automated pipeline runs and publishes a new post containing:

- **Fresh word discoveries** from Merriam-Webster, Wordnik, and manual contributions
- **Words promoted** from the invalid list after validation
- **Detailed statistics** including word-length and starting-letter distributions with interactive charts
- **Transparent execution logs** so you can see exactly what happened behind the scenes
- **Changelogs** and links to the latest Hugging Face release

## Why This Blog Matters

The English OpenList project has always been about **transparency, quality, and continuous improvement**. This blog makes that process visible and engaging for everyone — whether you're a developer building spell-checkers, a word-game enthusiast, a linguist, or just someone who loves language.

## How It Works

Our existing daily GitHub Action now does more than ever:

1. Discovers new words from multiple authoritative sources
2. Validates ~1,000 words from the invalid list
3. Updates the full dataset on Hugging Face
4. **Generates this beautiful daily blog post automatically**

This also ensures the repository stays active, so the automation never sleeps.

## What's Next?

We're just getting started. In the coming weeks you'll see:

- Richer statistics and visualizations
- Guest posts and community spotlights
- Deeper dives into interesting linguistic patterns
- Tools and notebooks built on top of English OpenList

## Get Involved

- **Add new words**: Submit them via `manual_additions.txt` in the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/ryanjosephkamp/english-openlist)
- **Follow updates**: Star the repo and watch the Actions tab
- **Use the data**: Download the latest release from [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ryanjosephkamp/english-openlist)

Thank you for being part of this project. Language is one of humanity's greatest tools — let's keep improving how we understand and use it together.

— The English OpenList Team
